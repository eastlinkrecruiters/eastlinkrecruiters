Application Protocol

Our streamlined digital process removes the need for paperwork.

1

Category Selection

Select a job tier that matches your career goals and current subscription plan. Upgrade if necessary to access Pro/Guaranteed listings.

2

Direct Application

Click the "Apply Directly" button. This is the recommended method to ensure your digital profile is routed immediately to the hiring manager.

3

Profile & Payment

Ensure your Digital Profile is 100% complete (Bio, Skills, Experience). For Premium jobs, complete the secure MPESA processing fee payment.

4

Confirmation & Tracking

Accept Terms & Conditions. Submit application. Track status in real-time via the "Applied Jobs" dashboard. No file uploads required.

Digital Profile Optimization

EastLink uses a modern, data-driven matching system. We do not use traditional file uploads. Your Digital Profile is your key to success.

How to Score Higher:
  • Fill out the "Bio" section with relevant keywords.
  • List all skills (soft & hard skills).
  • Ensure your contact details are verified.
  • Upload a clear, professional headshot.

Understanding the Processing Fee

EastLink Recruiters charges a processing fee for premium tiers to ensure quality and safety. This is a service fee, not a job purchase fee. It strictly covers:

  • Verification: Vetting employers to prevent scams.
  • Optimization: Administrative costs for profile enhancing (Pro tier).
  • Logistics: Platform maintenance and direct follow-up calls.

All listing tiers require a processing fee to ensure verification.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is placement guaranteed for Standard jobs?
Standard jobs are competitive. While we verify employers, placement depends on your qualifications. For guaranteed placement, consider the Guaranteed/Pro tier.
How long does the Guaranteed placement take?
For the Guaranteed/Pro tier, our target turnaround time is less than 1 week. This includes profile vetting and matching with our partner network.
Do I need to upload a CV file?
No. EastLink is a digital-first platform. We use the data in your "My Profile" section to match you with employers. Please ensure your digital profile is detailed and accurate.
