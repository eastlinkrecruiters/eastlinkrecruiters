Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a profile?
Click on "Join Now" in the menu, select "Job Seeker", and fill in your details. You can upload your CV immediately after signing up.
Are there fees for job seekers?
Basic registration and applying for jobs is free. We offer a "Pro Seeker" plan and "Guaranteed Placement" services which are paid premium features.
How can I post a job as an employer?
Create an Employer account, go to your dashboard, and select "Post a Job". You can choose between standard and premium posting options.
Where are your offices located?
We are located in Westlands, Nairobi, Kenya. Visits are currently by appointment only to ensure the best service.
